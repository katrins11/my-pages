Finndu Leið

Finndu leið or find a route is a webpage with running, hiking and cycling routes in Iceland. The web is for people who love training outdoors in our beautiful nature. We have Instagram to show how beautiful the route can be and to motivate people to inspire others.The point is to be able to go in and find routs close to you and log in to put your favorate route in to share with others. This project was a gratuation project in Reykjavik Academy of Web Development. We got nominated for top 5 best webs under the categroy community webs in the Web awards 2017 in Iceland.

Inside to TechTornato

The company Tech Tornado ( ctive) who organizes tech-related events has until now used Facebook as their main platform in order to distribute and make awareness about upcoming events. They have realized that they have technical needs that Facebook cannot ful fill. Therefore Tech Tornado wants an independent web application where they are able to manage their events. Tech Tornado wishes to have a web application where the users have a total overview of all the events. In addition to that the web application should have an administrator’s page, which includes functionalities that visualize statistics about the events.
The web application has two main goals:
• Users can see a clear overview of upcoming events and buy tickets.
• Users and administrators can create events and advertise.
